Why Use QR Attendance?

Fast & Efficient

Students can mark attendance in seconds with a quick QR code scan.

Secure & Reliable

Ensures accurate attendance records and prevents proxy attendance.

Automated Reports

Lecturers can generate real-time reports for attendance tracking.

How It Works

  1. Lecturers generate a unique QR code for each session.
  2. Students scan the QR code to confirm their attendance.
  3. Attendance is recorded and stored automatically.
  4. Both students and lecturers can access past attendance records.
