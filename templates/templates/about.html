QR Attendance Management System

About the QR Attendance Management System

The QR Attendance Management System is an innovative platform that automates attendance tracking using QR codes. It ensures accuracy, efficiency, and security in recording attendance for students and lecturers.

Key Features

  • QR Code-Based Attendance: Students scan unique QR codes to mark their attendance.
  • Real-Time Data: Attendance records are updated instantly and securely stored.
  • Automated Reports: Lecturers can generate detailed attendance analytics.
  • Secure Authentication: Only authorized users can access attendance data.

Our Mission

Our goal is to provide an easy-to-use, secure, and efficient attendance tracking system that eliminates manual errors and enhances classroom management.
